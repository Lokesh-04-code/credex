# Landing Copy — AI Spend Audit

---

## Hero Section

### Primary Headline
**"Stop Overpaying for AI Tools"**

*Rationale*: Direct, problem-focused, immediately understood by the ICP. Avoids jargon. Creates mild anxiety (are we overpaying?) that drives action.

### Alternative Headlines Tested
- "AI Tool ROI, Calculated in 3 Minutes" — too corporate
- "Find the Hidden Waste in Your AI Stack" — good but 'waste' sounds harsh
- "Your Team's AI Spending, Audited" — clear but passive

### Subheadline
**"Free 3-minute audit for founders and engineering managers. Find overspend, bad plans, and tool overlap."**

*(22 words — within the ≤25 word limit)*

*Rationale*: Specificity builds trust. "3 minutes" removes friction. "Bad plans" and "tool overlap" are concrete and relatable pain points.

## CTA Copy

### Primary CTA
**"Audit My AI Spend"**

*Rationale*: Action + personal pronoun. Feels like the user's own initiative, not a vendor pitch.

### Alternatives Considered
- "Get My Free Audit" — good, but "get" is weaker than "audit"
- "Start Free Audit" — used in nav/secondary CTAs (less personal)
- "Analyze My Spend" — works but "audit" implies authority/rigor

### Secondary CTA
**"See How It Works"**

*Used for*: Users who need more convincing before jumping into the form.

## FAQs

### Q: Is my data private?
Yes. Your email is only collected after you choose to receive results. The public share URL strips all personal data — it only shows tools and savings.

### Q: How accurate are the savings estimates?
Savings are calculated using official vendor pricing pages (sources documented in PRICING_DATA.md). We use deterministic business logic — not AI — for all financial math.

### Q: Do I need to connect any accounts?
No integrations required. You manually enter your tools, plans, and spend. This makes the tool work for any team structure, billing setup, or tool combination.

### Q: What AI tools are supported?
Cursor, GitHub Copilot, Claude, ChatGPT, Anthropic API, OpenAI API, Gemini, and Windsurf/v0. More tools added regularly.

### Q: Is this free?
The audit tool is completely free. For teams with >$500/month in savings, we surface an optional consultation with the Credex team who specialize in AI infrastructure optimization.
